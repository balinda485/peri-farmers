About PeriFarmers

Building Sustainable Poultry Farming In Uganda

PeriFarmers was established with a vision of creating a reliable, sustainable, and community-driven poultry farm that supports food security, employment, and local economic growth.

Through modern poultry farming techniques and responsible agricultural practices, we aim to produce quality poultry products while empowering surrounding farming communities.
